THE ATHENIANS INSTITUTE

We love Technology

No matter what your background is, you can learn skills that employers and clients need. Welcome to Athenians Institute. Premier Disruptive Technology Academy in Africa raising the next generation of innovators using project-based learning system to solve problems through AI, Data science, Software Development, Internet of Things(IoT), Robotics, Blockchain, and Web Technologies 2.0 . In the year 2022, Athenians Institute will celebrate its tenth anniversary. Over the last decade, technology has advanced dramatically, but one thing that hasn't changed is the industrious personnel at Athenians Institute who have kept up. Our objective when we started in 2012 was simple: to bring together a broad collection of students from all backgrounds to contribute to the expanding tech community.
